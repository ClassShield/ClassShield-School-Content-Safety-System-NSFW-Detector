Ethical AI Policy

ClassShield (Powered By AnveshAI) is designed with ethical AI principles at its core, prioritizing student safety, privacy, and fairness.

Core Ethical Principles

1. No Automatic Deletion

The system NEVER automatically deletes or removes content. All flagged items are sent to a human review queue where trained administrators make final decisions. This prevents false positives from causing unintended harm.

  • All suspect and harmful classifications trigger admin review
  • Students' work is preserved until human verification
  • Appeals process available for all decisions
2. Human-in-the-Loop Decision Making

AI serves as a tool to assist human moderators, not replace them. Trained school staff maintain final authority over all moderation decisions.

  • Administrators review all flagged content
  • Context-aware decision making by humans
  • Educational opportunities from borderline cases
3. Privacy by Design

Student privacy is paramount. Images are processed in memory and not permanently stored unless there's an active incident requiring investigation.

  • Images scanned in real-time without storage
  • Only cryptographic hashes retained for audit trails
  • Metadata stored with minimal personally identifiable information
  • Compliance with FERPA and student privacy laws
4. Explainability & Transparency

Every detection decision includes detailed explanations showing which models were used, confidence scores, and thresholds applied.

  • Clear reasoning for each decision
  • Confidence scores displayed for all detections
  • Threshold transparency
  • Method disclosure (local model vs. cloud API)
5. Bias Mitigation & Fairness

The system has been tested for bias across different demographics to ensure fair treatment of all students.

  • Tested on diverse skin tones and body types
  • Regular audits for disparate impact
  • Continuous monitoring of false positive rates by demographic
  • See our Bias Testing Report for details
6. Educational Purpose Over Surveillance

This system exists to protect students and create teachable moments, not to surveil them.

  • Focus on education and safety, not punishment
  • Opportunities for digital citizenship education
  • Restorative justice approaches when appropriate

Implementation Safeguards

  • Multi-tier detection: Local model, cloud fallback, and heuristic methods
  • Conservative thresholds: Designed to minimize false positives
  • Audit logging: All actions tracked for accountability
  • Regular review: Policy and threshold updates based on performance data
  • Staff training: Comprehensive training for all administrators

Limitations & Disclaimers

This system is NOT perfect and should be viewed as an assistive tool:
  • False positives and false negatives will occur
  • Context cannot always be understood by AI
  • Artistic, educational, or medical content may be incorrectly flagged
  • Human judgment remains essential
